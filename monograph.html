Talestagram

My project based on Instagram, a smartphone app that allow users to edit and upload pictures to a shared online space with their friends and followers. I created a username called bethereorbesquared2013 and follow 10 strangers' accounts. I go through my news feed, pick out a 17 sequential pictures to make a children storybook. I printed out the photos and text lines, and then pasted them on colored watercolor papers. I wanted to play back and forth with the idea of digital pictures that should not be printed out, real photos from strangers that made up a fake story and a children book that was not for children.
