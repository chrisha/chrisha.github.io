Become The Master

I designed an instruction board teaching people how to make an origami butterfly. I took photographs of different steps in the progress of folding the butterfly, and used Photoshop to add guidelines to the pictures.





After the testing section, one out of three testers succeeded making the object following the instruction I designed. Despite the bias about age, personal interests and references of the testers, I concluded that my design works, even though the presentation is not yet friendly and compelling to the users.
